{% trans %} Ckan supports two types of search: {% endtrans %}

  • Simple Search
  • advanced search

{%trans%} Simple Search Rules {%endtrans%}:

  • {%trans%} The search words are optional by default
    Ex: Oxygen → all datasets that have the word "oxygen" {%endtrans%}
  • {%trans%} Mandatory word → +
    Ex: oxygen +free → datasets that contain "oxygen" and must have the word "free" {%endtrans%}
  • {%trans%} Prohibited word → -
    Ex: oxygen -free → datasets that contain "oxygen" and excluded the ones with the word "free" {%endtrans%}
  • {%trans%} Phrase → " "
    Ex: "oxygen free" → datasets that contain "oxygen free" as a phrase {%endtrans%}
  • {%trans%} Stemming. Ckan search engine (solr) uses stemming on a search query
    Ex: search for "produce" also searches for "producing" and "production" {%endtrans%}
  • {%trans%} Does NOT support wild card search
    {%endtrans%}

{%trans%} Advanced Search Rules {%endtrans%}:

  • {%trans%} The advanced search get enabled if the user utilize colon in the search query
    title:chemistry → all datasets that have the word "chemistry" in their title {%endtrans%}
  • {%trans%} Wild card search → *
    title:chem* → all datasets that have the words which start with "chem" such as "Chemistry" or "Chemical" {%endtrans%}
  • {%trans%} OR → ||
    title:chemistry || title:bio → all datasets that have the word "chemistry" OR "bio" in their title {%endtrans%}
  • {%trans%} Proximity → ~
    title:"chemistry bio" ~ 4 → all datasets that have the word "chemistry" and "bio" in their title with 4 words in between (chemistry XX AA BB KK bio) {%endtrans%}
  • {%trans%} Fuzzy searches based on the Levenshtein Distance:
    author: powell~ → searches for words in close distance to the search term such as “jowell” or “pomell” {%endtrans%}
